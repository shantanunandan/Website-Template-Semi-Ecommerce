Know About Subhanpur

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Subhanpur village is 257666. Subhanpur village is located in Kashi Chak Tehsil of Nawada district in Bihar, India. It is situated 6km away from sub-district headquarter Kashi Chak and 40km away from district headquarter Nawada. As per 2009 stats, Subhanpur village is itself a gram panchayat. The total geographical area of village is 290 hectares. Subhanpur has a total population of 1,459 peoples. There are about 260 houses in Subhanpur village. Warisaliganj is nearest town to Subhanpur which is approximately 12km away.

Geographical Location

Subhanpur is a Village in Kashichak Tehsil in Nawada District of Bihar State, India. It is located 36 KM towards South-West from District head quarters Nawada. 48KM from Bihar Sharif. 120KM from State capital Patna. Subhanpur Pin code is 805124 and postal head office is Bhatta . Subhanpur is surrounded by Dhota towards East , Bhatta towards North , Tharpos towards South , Dhanpur and Dihri towards West . Bihar Sharif , Nawada , Lakhisarai , Begusarai , Gaya , Jahanabad are the nearby Cities to Subhanpur.

Population

Subhanpur is a medium size village located in Nawada district, Bihar with total 118 families residing. The Subhanpur village has population of 1,459 of which 807 are males while 652 are females as per Population Census 2011. In Subhanpur village population of children with age 0-6 is 148 which makes up 6.97% of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Subhanpur village is 10:8(Male:Female). Child Sex Ratio for the Subhanpur as per census is 846, equal than Bihar average of 846. Subhanpur village has higher literacy rate compared to other villages in Bihar. In 2011, literacy rate of Subhanpur village was 62.02 % compared to 43.85 % of nearby villages. In Subhanpur Male literacy stands at 88.05 % while female literacy rate was 79.72 %. As per constitution of India and Panchyati Raaj Act, Subhanpur village is administrated by Sarpanch (Head of Village) and Mukhiya who are elected representative of village. In Subhanpur village out of total population, 908 were engaged in work activities. 84% of working population were involved in Marginal activity providing livelihood for more than 2 years. 200 workers out of 908 engaged in Main Work, 596 were cultivators (owner or co-owner) while 112 were Agricultural labourer.
